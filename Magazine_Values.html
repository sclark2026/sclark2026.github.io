Values and Light

Importance

Values and light are fundamentals that are very intertwined with composition, color, and form.

Using light is difficult without understanding how it scatters, reflects on, and passes though different forms. How an object's color reacts to light is also heavily reliant on the warmth or coolness of the light being produced.

Composition is heavily reliant on values. Levels of brightness can control how different aspects of your illustration contrast and blend in against each other. It is also a fantastic way to study negative space, especially if you attempt painting with limited shades.
