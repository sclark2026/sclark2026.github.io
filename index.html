Exercises

Use References

Observe your surroundings! Found any particularly awestriking artists lately? Take a moment to look at their art and study their style!

Draw

This one is a no-brainer. Find the time to draw every day; even doodling will do! Don't let your skills go rusty.

Watch Tutorials

Scrolling through your Youtube recommended page but unable to find anything that interests you? Look up a tutorial about whatever art topic that interests you! Use your resources to your advantage.

Figure Drawing

Use the website Line of Action! Practice those pen strokes with a two-minute timer! Challenge yourself to capture a pose within limited time!
